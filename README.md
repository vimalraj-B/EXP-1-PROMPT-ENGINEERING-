## Aim: 
Comprehensive Report on the Fundamentals of Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs)
Experiment: Develop a comprehensive report for the following exercises:

Explain the foundational concepts of Generative AI.
Focusing on Generative AI architectures. (like transformers).
Generative AI applications.
Generative AI impact of scaling in LLMs.

## Algorithm:



**Prompt:**

1.Write a structured academic report titled "PROMPT ENGINEERING – Fundamentals of Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs)”*.

2.Begin with an introduction defining Generative AI and distinguishing it from traditional AI with examples (text, image, audio). 

3.Explain core concepts including Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and probability-based generation. 

4.Describe types of generative models such as GANs, VAEs, Diffusion Models, and Transformers. 

5.Explain the Transformer architecture with a simple flow diagram and key components like self-attention and positional encoding. 

6.Include applications of Generative AI across NLP, computer vision, software development, and business. 

7.Define LLMs and explain how they work with a step-by-step diagram. 

8.Describe key components like tokens, embeddings, attention, and parameters. 

9.Explain training stages including pre-training, fine-tuning, and RLHF. 

10.Discuss capabilities of LLMs such as text generation and translation. 

11.Explain the concept of scaling with its impact, benefits, and challenges. 

12.Include scaling laws, emergent abilities, and trade-offs. 

13.Conclude with advantages, limitations, and real-world applications.
